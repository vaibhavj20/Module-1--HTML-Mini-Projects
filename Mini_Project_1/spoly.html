Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering is a Professional Engineering discipline that deals with the design, construction and maintenance of the physically and naturally built environment, including works such as bridges, roads, canals, dams and buildings. Civil Engineering is the oldest Engineering Discipline after Military Engineering It is traditionally broken into different disciplines including Environmental Engineering, Structural Engineering, Transportation Engineering, Water Resource Engineering, Geo-technical Engineering, Material Engineering, Survey Engineering and Construction Engineering. Civil Engineering takes places at all levels in the public sector and private sector.
